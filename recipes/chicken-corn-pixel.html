chicken corn chowder
♡ best when served with a side of garlicy bread ♡
4 servings
ingredients needed
✲ 2 strips bacon
✲ 4 chicken thighs
✲ dried italian herb mix
✲ 1 tbsp tomato paste
✲ 3 cups chicken broth
✲ 1-2 potatoes
✲ 1 cup frozen corn
✲ 3/4 cup half & half
✲ 4oz cottage cheese (optional)
✲ 1/4 cup cheddar cheese
✲ pepper, to taste
✲ salt, to taste
steps
1
season chicken with garlic powder, dried herbs, and salt
2
cook the bacon and reserve the drippings
3
sear the chicken
4
stir in tomato paste
5
add broth and potato
6
let simmer for 30min-1h
7
add frozen corn
8
blend half & half with cottage cheese
9
stir in half & half and cheddar cheese
10
serve!
