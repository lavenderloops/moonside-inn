resources

honor mode tips

take your time

remember that there's never any rush to make a decision once you're in a fight - sometimes it even helps to save mid-fight and come back to it later. if things are going poorly and you rush your decisions, chances are it will only get worse!

have an exit plan

especially early game - try to keep at least one invisibility potions/scroll on you at all times, when in doubt, run and come back later!

leave someone behind

if you feel like you may be under-leveled for an encounter, it doesn't hurt to send one party member back to camp. if things go poorly, you'll have someone on standby to res your team. if it's going well, you can bring them back to finish the fight

all the summons

bring lots of summons! it is always better to have more friends in the initiative order, it helps take some pressure off your party members. classes that get summons pretty early on are beastmaster ranger, wizard, and pact of the chain warlock. cleric also get planar ally as a level 6 spell.

camp concerts

i like to have at least 2 hirelings at camp at all time, pretty much as soon as i can get them from withers. you can also use origin characters for these roles if you don't plan to take them out of camp too often. the idea is to buff your party every morning with any spells that don't require concentration. they're also super helpful if you need to heal mid-day and don't want to use a short rest/potions/spell slots.
