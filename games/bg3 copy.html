honor mode tips

  • take your time - remember that there's never any rush to make a decision once you're in a fight - sometimes it even helps to save mid-fight and come back to it later. if things are going poorly and you rush your decisions, chances are it will only get worse!
  • have an exit plan - especially in early game! and try to keep at least one invisibility potions/scroll on you at all times, when in doubt, run away and try again later!
  • leave someone behind - if you feel like you may be under-leveled for an encounter, it doesn't hurt to send one party member back to camp. if things go poorly, you'll have someone on standby to res your team. if it's going well, you can bring them back to finish the fight
  • slightly dishonor mode - this won't work if you get a TKO, but if there's a story moment that goes terribly wrong you can always ctl+alt+delete out of there (i suppose this is slightly less honorable, but sometimes its gotta happen! at the end of the day its your game, and you should feel free to play in whichever way is most fun)
