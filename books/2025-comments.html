i'm soo glad i still had the audiobook saved for my 15+ hours of holiday travelling. this plus stardew valley are the only way i could have made it through!

I feel like S1 of the show and the first book were pretty close in how they interpreted everything, but this one was sooo different! while i did feel like it dragged on a little bit in the middle of the first book, i really appreciated some of the slower moments that the show rushed/completely skipped in this one. especially the introduction of Selene/Lanfear it was soo fun and ominous how she always appeared just to like nudge Rand in whatever direction she wanted.
