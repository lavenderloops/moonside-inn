buldak ramen + corn cheese

a weekly meal

ingredients

  • carbonara buldak
  • corn (frozen or canned or fresh)
  • cheese (i like mozz)
  • mayo
  • sugar
  • onion (green or white)
  • bacon and/or lil smokies
  • milk

steps

  1. add onion and corn to frying pan with some oil or butter, let cook til onions are soft
  2. lower heat and add mayo, cheese, and sugar
  3. transfer to an oven save dish and bake in 400F oven until golden and bubbly or the rest of the ramen is ready
  4. cook bacon and/or lil smokies in same pan you made cheese corn
  5. Pour in water and bring to a boil before adding buldak noodles
  6. let simmer for a while then strain off about 80% of the water
  7. add a splash of milk along with seasoning packets and let the rest of the liquid simmer off
  8. scoop some corn cheese on top of noodles and serve
